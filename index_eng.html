Meow Facts - is a fun and informative web application created for all cat lovers. Every time you click on the "Click on me!" button, you will be presented with an interesting and unusual fact about cats. Why you should try Meow Facts:
  • Learn something new: Each fact is a little discovery that will help you better understand these amazing animals.
  • Lift Your Mood: Funny and cute facts about cats always lift your mood.
  • Get away from it all: A few minutes with Meow Facts is a great way to relax and unwind.
